About SBI

State Bank of India (SBI), a Fortune 500 company, is a multinational public sector banking and financial services statutory body headquartered in Mumbai. With a legacy spanning over 200 years, it is India's most trusted bank.

Our Network

SBI serves over 450 million customers through its vast network of:

  • 22,000+ branches across India
  • 62,000+ ATMs nationwide
  • 200+ offices in 30 foreign countries

Our Foundation

SBI's origins date back to the establishment of the Bank of Calcutta in 1806. Through a series of mergers, it became the State Bank of India in 1955, growing into an integral part of India's economic landscape.
